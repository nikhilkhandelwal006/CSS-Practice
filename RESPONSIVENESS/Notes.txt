1. An absolute units of measurements i ssomething that is fixed in length.
        It doesn't change based on screen time.
2.Never use pixels.

3. A relative units of measurements is not fixed in length but rather it is determined relative to another units of measurements.
4. 1vw- 1% of the width od userscreen/viewport
5. 1vh- 1% of the height of userscreen/viewport
6. 1%- 1% relative to parent element.
7. Never use pixels - use em!
8. em is relative to current font size of the element
9. 2.5em is 2.5x to current font size.
10. 3em is 3x to current font size.
